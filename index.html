FAQ

Before you book.

Quick answers about access, weather, parking and timing, so the booking feels clear from the start.

Do you bring water and power?

Yes, the mobile setup is designed to be self-contained. If a safe outdoor tap or mains power is available, it can be used, but the service does not depend on it for most packages.

What happens if it rains?

Light rain is usually manageable, especially for interior work and maintenance valets. Heavy rain or weather that would compromise the result can be rearranged.

Can you work in apartments or underground parking?

In many cases, yes. The key requirements are safe access, permission to work, enough space around the vehicle and parking for the mobile setup nearby.

How long does a detail take?

Maintenance valets can be completed faster, while full details, paint enhancement and ceramic coatings take longer. The quote will include an estimated time window.

Do you only work on prestige cars?

No. We work on everyday cars, family cars, work vehicles, new cars and prestige vehicles. The standard is careful professional car care, whatever you drive.

Do you offer ceramic coating and paint correction?

Yes. Ceramic coating starts from £250.00 and is available after an enhancement detail or paint correction, because the paint needs proper preparation first. Paint correction is quoted after assessment.
